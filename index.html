Filmeto — AI-powered Storyboard Software

Filmeto — AI-powered storyboard software. Quickly convert scripts into shot language, storyboard canvas, and deliverable shot lists.

Turn stories into shots, turn shots into deliverable storyboards.

Filmeto uses AI to automatically generate storyboard suggestions from scripts/outline, and quickly organize shot sequences, notes, reviews and exports on the canvas, suitable for short films, advertising, animation and content creation workflows.

AI Auto Storyboarding: From Script to Shot Language

Break down long texts into executable shots: rhythm, framing, movement, and emotional cues are provided together, and can be rewritten with one click at any time.

  • Shot Suggestions: Automatically provide framing/angle/movement
  • Rhythm Control: Support fast-cut/long-take styles
  • Editable: Each frame can be fine-tuned separately

Storyboard Canvas: Drag-and-Drop Layout and Shot Sequences

Arrange shots like building with LEGO: drag-and-drop sorting, grouping, labeling shot numbers and durations to maintain creative flow.

  • Drag Sorting: Shot order and grouping changed by dragging
  • Labeling Info: Duration, shot number, scene, notes
  • Multiple Views: Freely switch between grid/timeline/list

Consistency Control: Keeping Characters/Scenes/Props On Track

Establish project-level visual and narrative settings to make every generation closer to your world view and style.

  • Settings Library: Unified management of characters, scenes, props
  • Style Anchors: Color tone/shot language/emotional baseline
  • Constrained Generation: Avoid conflicts in character settings and scenes

Shot Comments and Collaboration: More Efficient Review

Put comments on shots: comment frame by frame, @collaborators, mark modification points to make the review process more traceable.

  • Frame-by-frame Comments: Each frame is a discussion context
  • Version Comparison: Changes visible at a glance
  • Export and Share: Share links or export review packages

Structured Breakdown: Traceable Link from Paragraphs to Shots

Each shot can be traced back to the original paragraph and intention, facilitating explanation and review of why shoot this way.

  • Traceable: Bidirectional jump between shots and text paragraphs
  • Tag System: Emotion, location, character, action
  • Quick Reorganization: Batch adjustment by tags/scenes

One-click Export: Delivery Formats Cover Common Workflows

For production and communication: quickly deliver storyboards to upstream and downstream artists, directors, editors and producers.

  • Common Formats: PDF/PNG sequence/CSV shot list
  • Resolution: Support HD and print versions
  • Extensible: More delivery templates can be accessed subsequently

Filmeto — AI 驱动的 Storyboard 分镜

Filmeto — AI 驱动的 storyboard 分镜软件。把剧本快速转为镜头语言、分镜画布与可交付的镜头表。

把故事变成镜头，把镜头变成可交付的分镜。

Filmeto 通过 AI 帮你从剧本/大纲自动生成分镜建议，并在画布中快速组织镜头序列、备注、评审与导出，适用于短片、广告、动画与内容创作流程。

AI 自动分镜：从剧本到镜头语言

把长文本拆成可执行的镜头：节奏、景别、运动、情绪提示一并给出，并且可随时一键重写。

  • 镜头建议：自动给出景别/角度/运动
  • 节奏控制：支持快剪/长镜头风格
  • 可编辑：每一格都能单独微调

Storyboard 画布：拖拽排版与镜头序列

像搭乐高一样排镜头：拖拽排序、分组、标注镜头号与时长，保持创作流畅。

  • 拖拽排序：镜头顺序与分组一拖即改
  • 标注信息：时长、镜头号、场次、备注
  • 多视图：网格/时间线/列表自由切换

一致性控制：角色/场景/道具保持不跑偏

建立项目级"视觉与叙事设定"，让每次生成都更贴近你的世界观与风格。

  • 设定库：角色、场景、道具统一管理
  • 风格锚点：色调/镜头语言/情绪基线
  • 约束生成：避免人物设定与场景冲突

镜头备注与协作：评审更高效

把意见落在镜头上：逐格评论、@协作者、标记修改点，让评审过程更可追溯。

  • 逐格评论：每一格都是讨论的上下文
  • 版本对比：改动点一眼可见
  • 导出共享：分享链接或导出评审包

结构化拆解：从段落到镜头的可追踪链路

每个镜头都能回溯到原始段落与意图，方便做"为什么这样拍"的解释与复盘。

  • 可追踪：镜头 ↔ 文本段落双向跳转
  • 标签体系：情绪、地点、人物、动作
  • 快速重排：按标签/场次批量调整

一键导出：交付格式覆盖常见流程

面向制作与沟通：把分镜快速交给美术、导演、剪辑与制片的上下游。

  • 常用格式：PDF/PNG 序列/CSV 镜头表
  • 分辨率：支持高清与打印版
  • 可扩展：后续可接入更多交付模板
